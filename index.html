ABOUT

Named after its lyrical concept and feeling, Pale Circus is an alternative music band that thrives unique sounds. After Ege spent a lot of time on different genres and finding new sonics and sounds at the beginning of 2020, Ege finally started to write the debut album. They now write new music and perform as a four-piece band with Cem on drums, Dersu on keys and guitars, Doğuhan on bass guitar and synth bass, and Ege on guitars and vocals. Inspired by genres such as neo-psych, shoegaze, psychedelic rock, dream pop, and indie pop, they create new music and singles while working together on a new album with an evolved musical approach and perspective.
